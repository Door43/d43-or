===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **all his brothers** - this includes 10 older brothers and 1 younger brother 
    * **were fruitful** - This means that the Israelite population grew quickly. AT: "had many children" or "gave birth to many children" (UDB). (See: [[or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **the land was filled with them** - AT: "They filled the land" (See: [[or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
    * **with them** - The word "them" refers to the Israelites.
