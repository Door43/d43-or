===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **what was evil in Yahweh's sight** - See how you translated this phrase in [[or:bible:notes:jdg:02:11|2:11]].
  * **The power of Midian oppressed Israel** - Midian is referred to by the aspect of its culture that allowed it to oppress the people of Israel. AT: "Midian was more powerful than Israel and oppressed them" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **dens** - places in the rocky cliffs that would provide shelter.
