===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he had buried him** - Here the word "he" refers to the old prophet and the word "him" refers to the man of God.
  * **when he cried out** - AT: "when he prophesied loudly" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
