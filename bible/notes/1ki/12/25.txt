===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Jeroboam built Shechem** - AT: "Jeroboam's workers built Shechem" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **thought in his heart** - AT: "thought to himself" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **the house of David** - AT: "the kings descended from David"
  * **If these people go up** - The words "these people" refer to the people of the northern ten tribes of Israel.
  * **turn again to their master, to Rehoboam king of Judah...return to Rehoboam king of Judah** - These phrases mean the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
