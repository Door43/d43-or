===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now therefore** - Isaiah uses these words to emphasize what he is about to say.
  * **your bonds will be tightened** - AT: "Yahweh will tighten your bonds" or "Yahweh will punish you even more severely" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[or:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **a decree of destruction on the earth** - AT: "that he is going to destroy people throughout the land." (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
