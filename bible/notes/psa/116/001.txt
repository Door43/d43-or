===== Translation Notes: =====

This psalm is a song of thanksgiving.\\
See: [[or:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]
  * **I love** - The word "I" refers to the person who made this song. We do not know who this person was.
  * **my voice** - AT: "what I say." (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **will call on him**  - "call to him" or "pray to him"
