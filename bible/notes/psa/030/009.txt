===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **What advantage... your trustworthiness?** - David does not expect an answer to these questions.  (See:  [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Dust praise you** - "Dust" is a picture here of death or the body's having died.(See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
