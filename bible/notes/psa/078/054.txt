===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues to describe what God did for the people of Israel.

  * **this mountain that his right hand had acquired** - Here the term "right hand" is a symbol of God's power, authority, or strength. (See: Metonymy)
  * **he settled the tribes of Israel in their tents**  - The pronoun "their" refers to the nations God drove out of the land of Canaan.
  * **assigned them their inheritance**  - The word "inheritance" can also be translated as a verb: "assigned them their land to inherit"
