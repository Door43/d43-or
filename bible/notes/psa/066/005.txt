===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he, him,his **- Refers to God.
  * **he is fearsome in his deeds** - AT "he is mighty in his deeds"
  * **He turned the sea into dry land; they went through the river on foot;** - This refers the crossing the Red sea. (See:[[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **the children of men **- AT: "humanity"
  * **they, we **- These refer to God's people, Israelites. David includes himself in the rejoicing, so switches to "we".
