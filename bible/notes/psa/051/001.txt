===== Translation Notes: =====

See: Translating Poetry and [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **because of your covenant faithfulness; for the sake of the multitude of your merciful actions**  - AT: "because of your covenant love and many merciful actions" (See: Doublet)
  * **blot out my transgressions**  -AT: "cancel my transgressions"
  * **wash me thoroughly from my iniquity and cleanse me from my sin**  - AT: "wash me completely from my iniquity and cleanse me from my sin" (See: Doublet)
