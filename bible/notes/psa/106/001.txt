===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:or:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

This psalm is a song of praise.

  * **Who can recount the mighty acts of Yahweh ... ?**- The author asks this question to praise God and does not expect an answer. AT: "No one can recount the mighty acts of Yahweh ..." (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **praiseworthy deeds** - "deeds that are worthy of praise"
