===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he blessed his soul** - The rich man is referred to by referring to his inner self. AT: "he did good things for himself" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **live for yourself** - AT: "live to benefit yourself"
  * **they will never see the light again** - Being able to see the light of the sun is used to refer to being alive because the dead cannot see the sun. AT: "and they will live no more"
