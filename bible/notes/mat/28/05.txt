===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of the resurrection of Jesus from the dead.

  * **the women**  - "Mary Magdalene and the other woman named Mary"
  * **who has been crucified**  - "whom the people and the soldiers crucified" (See: [[or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but is risen**  - "but God has raised him" (See: Active/Passive)
