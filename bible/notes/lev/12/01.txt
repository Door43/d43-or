  * **during the days of her monthly period** - This refers to the time of the month when a woman bleeds from her womb. (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **the flesh of a baby boy's foreskin must be circumcised** - AT: "a priest must circumcise the baby boy" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
