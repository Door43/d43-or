===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so as to prostitute themselves with them** - Here "prostitute" means the people are unfaithful to Yahweh. AT: "so as to seek advise from them rather than from me" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **set my face against** - See how you translated this in [[or:bible:notes:lev:20:03|20:3]].
