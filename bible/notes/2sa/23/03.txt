===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Rock of Israel**  - A [[or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] describing the reliability and strength of God to Israel. This verse connects to the previous verse by [[or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]].
  * **me**  - Refers to David (See: [[or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
  * **He will be like the morning star**  - The phrase is a [[or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] describing the enlightening quality of a king. AT: The king who rules in the fear of God will lead his people in truth.
