===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Israel.
  * **Tamar...Meribah Kadesh...Brook of Egypt** - See how you translated these in [[or:bible:notes:ezk:47:18|47:19]].
  * **you** - This is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
  * **cast lots** - See how you translated this in [[or:bible:notes:ezk:45:01|45:1]].
  * **This is the Lord Yahweh's declaration** - See how you translated this in [[:or:bible:notes:ezk:05:11|5:11]].
