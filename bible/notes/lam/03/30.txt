===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer speaks of the one who waits for Yahweh ([[:or:bible:notes:lam:03:25|3:25]]).

    * **Let him give his cheek to the one striking him** - "Let him allow people to strike him" or  (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **though he brings** - "though Yahweh brings"
    * **he does not oppress from the heart** - AT: "He does not willingly oppress" or "He takes no delight in oppressing" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **sons of men** - AT: "human beings" or "people" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
