===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you have pleaded the disputes of my life** - God pleads his case as if he were a lawyer. AT: "you have pleaded my case" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **you have redeemed my life** - AT: "you have redeemed me" or "you have saved my life" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **their oppression of me** - AT: "how they oppress me" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **all their acts of vengeance, all their plans against me** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **all their acts of vengeance** - AT: "all of their attempts to take revenge against me" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **heard their scorn** - AT: "heard them taunt me" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
