===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **like a watered garden** - This means they will be strong and healthy, and they will prosper. (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **they will never again feel any more sorrow** - This is an exaggeration of the joy Israel will have. (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
