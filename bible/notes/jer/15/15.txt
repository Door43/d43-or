===== Translation Notes: =====

Jeremiah speaks to Yahweh.

  * **Call me to mind**  - AT: "Remember me" or "Consider me and my situation" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **my pursuers** - These are people who were trying to harm Jeremiah and were trying to find him.
  * **In your patience do not take me away.**  - Jeremiah is concerned that Yahweh's patience will enable Jeremiah's enemies to kill him.
  * **Your words have been found** - AT: "I have heard your message." (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **and I consumed them** - "and I understood your message"
