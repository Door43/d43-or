===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pashhur **- This is not the same Pashur that was mentioned in 20:1. This Pashhur was King Zedekiah's chief minister. (See: Translate Names)
  * **Seek advice from Yahweh on our behalf **- AT: "Please speak to Yahweh for us. Ask him if he will help us." (UDB)
  * **is making war on us **- "is attacking us"
  * **as in times past**  - The phrase "as in times past" means "as was done before" (See: Idiom)
  * **withdraw from us **- "leave us alone" or "not attack us"
