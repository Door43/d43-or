===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **for a thousand generations** - "for 1,000 generations" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **repays those who hate him to their face** - This means God will repay them quickly and openly so that they know God has punished them. (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **he will not be lenient on whoever hates him** - AT: "he will severely punish everyone who hates him" (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **I command you** - Moses is speaking to the people of Israel.
