===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **take his father's wife **- The phrase particularly refers to "take...for oneself" and "take...in marriage" here.
  * **not take away his father's marriage rights **- "not uncover his father's skirt", which means "not to disgrace his father" (See: [[:or:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
