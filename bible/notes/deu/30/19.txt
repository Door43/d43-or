===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I call**  - "I" is Moses, who continues speaking.
  * **I call heaven and earth to witness against you today**  - Moses treats the creation as if it were an eyewitness to his words. (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **heaven and earth** - These words together mean "all creation". (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
  * **obey his voice**  - AT: "obey him". (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **For he is your life and the length of your days**  - This means that Yahweh is the one who can give the Israelites life, and long life. (See: [[:or:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
