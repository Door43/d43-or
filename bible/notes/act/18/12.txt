===== Translation Notes: =====
 
  * **Gallio became governor of Achaia** - Achaia was a Roman province which Corinth was part of that is now modern southern Greece. (See: [[or:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **brought him before the judgment-seat** - The Jews took Paul by force and brought Paul before the court. Alternate translation: "took him to be judged by the governor" 
  * **contrary to the law** - The Jews were purposely making Paul's offenses against Jewish laws and customs sound like crimes against Roman law.
