===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Mattaniah...Mica...Zabdi...Asaph...Bakbukiah...Abda...Shammua...Galal...Jeduthun** - These are male names.  (See: [[or:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **his associates** - "his brothers"
  * **In all the holy city were 284 Levites** - "All the Levites in the holy city were 284" or "There were, in all, 284 Levites in the holy city." See how you translated numbers in [[or:bible:notes:neh:07:11|07:11-14]].
